CSCAN Disk Scheduling Algorithm

Overview

The CSCAN algorithm improves upon SCAN by treating the disk as a circular list. Instead of reversing direction after reaching the end, the disk arm jumps to the beginning and starts servicing requests again.

Advantages

  • Provides more uniform wait time compared to SCAN.
  • Fairer for requests across the disk surface.

Disadvantages

  • May result in longer total head movements due to the jump from end to start.
  • More complex than FCFS or simple SCAN.
