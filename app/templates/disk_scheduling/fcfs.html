FCFS Disk Scheduling Algorithm

Overview

FCFS is the simplest of all the Disk Scheduling Algorithms. In FCFS, the requests are addressed in the order they arrive in the disk queue. This method does not consider the position of the disk arm or seek time, so it may not always result in the most efficient usage of the disk arm.

Advantages

  • Simple to implement and understand.
  • Works well when requests are evenly distributed and close together.

Disadvantages

  • May lead to long waiting times if requests are far apart, leading to inefficient disk arm movement.
  • Does not prioritize urgent or short requests, leading to potential delays for some processes.
