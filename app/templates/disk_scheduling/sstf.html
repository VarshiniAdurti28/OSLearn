SSTF Disk Scheduling Algorithm

Overview

The Shortest Seek Time First (SSTF) scheduling algorithm prioritizes the request closest to the current position of the disk head. This minimizes seek time compared to First-Come-First-Serve (FCFS).

SSTF reduces the overall seek time but may cause starvation for requests located far from the current head position.

Advantages

  • Reduces seek time compared to FCFS.
  • Improves the average response time.

Disadvantages

  • Requests located far from the current head position may starve.
  • More complex to implement than FCFS.
